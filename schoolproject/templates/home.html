COLLEGE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY CHENGANNUR

College of science and technology, chengannur is a College synonymous with knowledge empowerment that established in the name of the Pattayil Kunjukunju Memorial Charitable Trust. It is situated in chengannur,Alappuzha District of Kerala state. CST was envisaged by our beloved Chairman Sri. K. Sadanandan to provide generations of students an opportunity for excellent technical and business education. It is approved by the AICTE and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, Trivandrum.
